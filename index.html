Yossi Gandelsman

I'm an incoming assistant professor at TTIC, starting in September 2026.

Currently, I am a research scientist at Reve.

I completed my Ph.D. at UC Berkeley, advised by Prof. Alexei Efros. Before that, I was a member of the Perception Team at Google Research (now Google-DeepMind). I completed my M.Sc. at the Weizmann Institute of Science, advised by Prof. Michal Irani, and a B.Sc. at the Open University of Israel.

If you're interested in joining my lab, please don't hesitate to reach out.
